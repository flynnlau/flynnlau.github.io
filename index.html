Lauren Flynn is an artist based in the Twin Cities. She studied math and studio art at Grinnell College, and this duality continues to inform her work and thoughts. She recently completed her MFA at the University of Minnesota. Flynn’s work has been exhibited locally at the White Page Gallery and Highpoint Center for Printmaking, and nationally at Yes Ma’am Projects in Denver, Colorado, the DeVos Art Museum in Marquette, Michigan and Basement Projects in Santa Ana, California. She is a co-director of Yeah Maybe Gallery in Minneapolis.
