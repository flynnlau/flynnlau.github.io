Lauren Flynn is an artist based in the Twin Cities. She studied math and studio art at Grinnell College, and this duality continues to inform her work and thoughts. She recently completed her MFA in sculpture and ceramics at the University of Minnesota. Flynn’s work comes about through the creation of structures which make room for and are transformed by play and coincidence. She sometimes sees her art practice as an ongoing attempt to become gentler and closer to the floor. Flynn is a co-director of Yeah Maybe Gallery in Minneapolis, collaborator of Andy Delany and mother of Alys Delany.
